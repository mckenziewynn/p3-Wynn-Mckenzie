Hey Look, It's Me!

Hello! I’m McKenzie Wynn and I’m a storyteller! Whether that’s through through filmmaking, scriptwriting, making TikToks or memes I love ceating characters or strylines that inspire and resonate with people!

Throughout my academic career I have advanced my skills in various areas including writing, editing, video production, communication and most importantly, storytelling. I believe storytelling has the power to influence change, build relationships and create emotional connections. And this is my dream; I want to create stories that resonate with an audience.

This is what drives me to create content that not only inspires others but is empowering to those on the other side of the screen. With a passion for people and all things media, I find myself happiest when creating stories that resonate with the human condition.
